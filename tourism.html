Overview

Tourism Business Case

The ebikemalaga.es website serves as the main hub for the company, bringing together several commercial activities and providing a challenge in terms of SEO. To create a high-quality, user-friendly website, I executed the following tasks:

  • User Personas & keyword research: I began by developing user personas and conducting comprehensive keyword research to identify potential customer search terms and determine the best marketing strategies, including SEO and social media, to attract their target audience.
  • Data analysis: Based on the user personas and keyword research, I analyzed the data to develop effective UX strategies for the website.
  • UX & UI design: I created a visually appealing and user-friendly design, incorporating the company's branding and style guidelines, with a responsive design optimized for desktop, tablet and mobile devices.
  • Frontend development: Using Bootstrap, I developed the frontend of the website, optimizing it for speed and performance.
  • API integration: I integrated booking management software and payment system to ensure the whole booking process could be made online.
  • User testing: I conducted survey and moderated testing to ensure the website usability was optimized for our target audience.
  • Data analysis: I compared the data before/after deployement to optimise the next interation.
  • Maintenance & version updates: I continued to maintain the website, making regular updates to ensure it remained up-to-date and functional, and incorporating new features and functionality as the business evolved.
Overall, the bikehiremalaga.es project was a success, providing the bike rental company with a modern, user-friendly website that has helped to increase online bookings and grow the business.
