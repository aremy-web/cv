eCommerce Case

Bicicletas Electricas Malaga is an e-commerce website focused on selling electric bicycles in the Malaga region and overall Spain. The company's goal was to create a functional and easy-to-use e-commerce platform that would allow customers to quickly and easily purchase electric bicycles.

To achieve this goal, I executed the following tasks:

  • User research: Conducted user and keyword research to identify the target audience and search terms.
  • UX Strategy development: Organized the product list and categories on the website based on the research. Simplified the checkout process for customers.
  • Web Content: Focused on providing clear and concise information about each product.
  • Frontend development: Using Bootstrap, I developed the frontend of the website, optimizing it for speed and performance.
  • User testing: I conducted moderated testing to ensure the website usability was optimized for our target audience.
  • Data analysis: I compared the data before/after deployement to optimise the next interation.
  • Maintenance & version updates: I continued to maintain the website, making regular updates
