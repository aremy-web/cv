Sustainable Transportation Case

VEM is a brand-new company and everything had to be done digitally in order to establish its presence in the market. This required a comprehensive digital strategy that involved designing a logo that adhered to specific guidelines, creating social media profiles, UX/UI design, frontend development, deployment, and ongoing maintenance and version updates.

To achieve this goal, I undertook the following tasks:

  • Logo design: This task involved following specific guidelines, including the brand's color scheme, typography, and overall aesthetic dictated by the company creator.
  • Creation of social media profiles: In order to establish a digital presence, I created profiles on various social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn. The goal was to reach a wider audience and engage with businesses on multiple platforms.
  • UX & UI design: The website needed to be functional, informative, and concise, with a B2B focus. A significant amount of work was done on the blog to improve SEO.
  • Frontend development: Once the UX and UI designs were validated, the next step was to develop the frontend using Bootstrap.
Based on the completed tasks, it can be inferred that the overall work was successful in establishing a strong digital presence from the beginning and achieving successful search engine positioning.
