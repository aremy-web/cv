About

I have several years of experience in the design of digital solutions. Over time, I developed a particular interest for B2B and PaaS products. I currently work as a fully integrated member of a scrum team in an Agile environment, collaborating closely with frontend and backend developers.

My previous experience as a frontend developer helps me to understand the technical requirements of a project and ease frontend tasks. I am used to design around strict business requirements with an emphasis on usability for the end users. I have been fortunate enough to work in several countries and in very different environments. This developed my observation and empathy skills. I didn't know then, but now I believe it's a huge advantage as a designer.
